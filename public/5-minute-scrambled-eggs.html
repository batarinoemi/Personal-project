5-Minute Scrambled Eggs

A quick and easy breakfast

Time: 5 mins

Portions: 1

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp butter or oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Optional: 1â€“2 tbsp shredded cheese, a handful of spinach, or 1 slice of toast

Instructions

  • Crack eggs into a bowl, add salt & pepper, and whisk until yolks and whites are fully combined.
  • Heat butter in a nonstick pan over medium heat. Pour in eggs. Stir gently with a spatula, scraping the edges to the center. Keep stirring occasionally until eggs are mostly set but still slightly soft.
  • Remove from heat (eggs will keep cooking slightly off the heat). Top with cheese or add spinach to wilt for 30 seconds. Serve with toast if desired.
