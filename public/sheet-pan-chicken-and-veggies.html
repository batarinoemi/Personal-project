Sheet-Pan Chicken and Veggies

Healthy food

Time: 30 mins

Portions: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Optional: ½ tsp paprika, garlic powder, or dried herbs

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Slice vegetables and lightly season chicken and veggies with olive oil, salt, pepper, and optional spices.
  • Place chicken and vegetables on a baking sheet in a single layer. Bake until chicken reaches an internal temp of 165°F (74°C) and veggies are tender.
  • Let chicken rest 2–3 minutes, then slice and serve with veggies.
