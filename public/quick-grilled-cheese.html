Quick Grilled Cheese

Quick and tasty

Time: 5 mins

Portions: 1

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tsp butter

Instructions

  • Prep (1 min): Butter one side of each bread slice. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides so the butter is on the outside.
  • Heat a pan over medium heat. Place the sandwich in the pan, butter side down. Cook 2–3 minutes until golden brown. Flip carefully and cook another 1–2 minutes.
  • Remove from pan, slice, and enjoy while warm. Optional: add tomato slices or a pinch of herbs.
