Garlic Butter Pasta

Quick and tasty

Time: 15 mins

Portions: 1

Ingredients

  • 75g dry pasta
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 garlic clove, minced (or ½ tsp garlic powder)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan, chili flakes, or fresh parsley

Instructions

  • Boil a pot of salted water. Add pasta and cook until al dente. Drain and reserve 1–2 tbsp pasta water.
  • In a pan over medium heat, melt butter. Add garlic and sauté for 30–60 seconds until fragrant (don’t burn it!).
  • Add cooked pasta to the pan, toss with butter & garlic. If too dry, add a splash of reserved pasta water. Season with salt, pepper, and optional toppings.
