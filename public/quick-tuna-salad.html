Quick Tuna Salad

Healthy and quick

Time: 5 mins

Portions: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tuna (in water or oil, ~120g / 4 oz)
  • 1–2 tbsp mayonnaise (or Greek yogurt for a lighter option)
  • ½ tsp mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional: chopped celery, onion, or pickles

Instructions

  • Drain the tuna and place it in a bowl.
  • Add mayonnaise, mustard (if using), salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined. Add optional veggies if desired.
  • Eat as is, on bread, in a wrap, or on a bed of lettuce.
