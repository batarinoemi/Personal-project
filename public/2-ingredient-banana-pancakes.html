2-Ingredient Banana Pancakes

Sweet and tasty

Time: 5 mins

Portions: 2-3 pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 eggs
  • Optional: pinch of cinnamon, 1 tsp peanut butter, or berries

Instructions

  • Mash the banana in a bowl until smooth. Beat in the eggs until fully combined. Add optional cinnamon or extras.
  • Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat. Lightly oil or butter the pan. Pour small portions of batter (about 2–3 inches wide) into the pan. Cook 1–2 minutes until edges set, then flip and cook another 1–2 minutes.
  • Stack pancakes, add toppings like peanut butter, syrup, or fruit, and enjoy!
